Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

At MetroGuideIndia, we are committed to maintaining the trust of our visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect and how we safeguard your data while you use our transit tools.

Information Collection & Use

  • Feedback & Support: When you use our contact form, we collect the information you provide (such as your email and message) via Formspree solely to respond to your inquiries or improve our data.
  • Usage Analytics: We may collect non-identifiable data, such as page views and browser types, to optimize the performance and user experience of our website.
  • Local Search: Route selections and station searches are processed locally to provide immediate results and are not stored on our permanent servers.

Cookies & Tracking

We may use essential cookies to ensure the website functions correctly. If we implement third-party analytics (like Google Analytics) or advertising in the future, we will update this policy accordingly. You can manage your cookie preferences through your individual browser settings.

Third-Party Processing

We do not sell or trade your personal information. However, we use trusted third-party services (such as form processors) to facilitate our communication. These providers have access to your info only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose it.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at: metroguideindiasupport@gmail.com
