Why this route is so popular

The Noida Electronic City to Rajiv Chowk route is one of the most used direct Blue Line journeys for office commuters. It connects Sector 62/63 and nearby IT clusters to central Delhi without a line change.

Rajiv Chowk exit and interchange guidance

  • Gate 5/6: Best for Janpath and Connaught Place central area.
  • Gate 2/3: Useful for PVR Plaza, B/H blocks and nearby commercial zones.
  • North Delhi connection: During peak crowding, many commuters switch at Mandi House based on destination convenience.

Commuter tips that save time

  • Board with interchange positioning in mind when approaching high-footfall hubs.
  • Expect signal fluctuation near transition points between elevated and underground sections.
  • Keep a small time buffer during 8–10 AM and 5–7 PM peak windows.
