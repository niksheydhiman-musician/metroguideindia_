Complete Namo Bharat RRTS Fare Guide 2026

The Delhi–Meerut Namo Bharat RRTS became fully operational on 22 February 2026. Here are the official verified fares sourced from NCRTC as of February 2026. All prices are for Standard Coach, one-way.

Premium Coach fares are approximately 20% higher than Standard. E.g. Sarai Kale Khan → Modipuram: ₹210 Standard / ₹250 Premium.

How to Pay

Tickets can be bought at station counters, Ticket Vending Machines (TVMs), or via the Namo Bharat Connect app. NCMC/RuPay Smart Cards enable tap-in tap-out across all stations.

Concessions

  • Children under 90 cm travel free on both RRTS and Meerut Metro
  • RRTS minimum fare: ₹20 (Standard), ₹30 (Premium)
  • Meerut Metro minimum fare: ₹10
  • NCMC Smart Card accepted at all stations
Source: NCRTC official data via meerutmetro.in, February 2026. Always verify current fares at the station or on the Namo Bharat Connect app before travel.
