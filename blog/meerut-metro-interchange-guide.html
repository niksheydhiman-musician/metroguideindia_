Meerut Metro Interchange Guide — All 4 Stations Explained

The Namo Bharat RRTS and Meerut Metro share four interchange stations, allowing passengers to switch between the two systems seamlessly. Here is a detailed guide for each.

Meerut South — Platform 2

Meerut South is the first interchange point as you arrive from Delhi. It is also the southernmost station of the Meerut Metro. Exit the RRTS platform and follow signs to Platform 2 for the Meerut Metro towards Partapur and beyond.

Shatabdi Nagar — Platform 1

Shatabdi Nagar is the most centrally located interchange and recommended for passengers heading to Meerut Central, Brahampuri, or Bhaisali Bus Adda. Follow signs to Platform 1 for the Meerut Metro.

Begumpul — Platform 3

Begumpul interchange connects to the Meerut Metro's mid-section, providing access to MES Colony and surrounding areas. Meerut Metro Platform 3 is signposted from the RRTS concourse.

Modipuram — Platform 1 & 2

Modipuram is both the northern terminus of the RRTS and a Meerut Metro station. Passengers can board the Meerut Metro from Platform 1 or Platform 2 at this station.
