Exit Gate Information

Exit gates at Srirampura are typically signposted for local roads, bus stops, markets, and institutions. Use this quick reference and confirm gate boards on-site for the latest wayfinding updates.

  • Gate 2: Hospital/clinic side and nearby residential blocks.
  • Gate 3: Bus stop, feeder services, and office corridor approach.
  • Gate 4: Mall/commercial lane, pickup-drop zone, and parking side access.

BMRCL parking availability near Srirampura is generally subject to local operations and peak-hour demand.
