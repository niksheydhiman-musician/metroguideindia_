Station Access Guide

Exit Gate Information

Exit gates at Electronic City are typically signposted for local roads, bus stops, markets, and institutions. Use this quick reference and confirm gate boards on-site for the latest wayfinding updates.

  • Gate 1: Main road access, auto-rickshaw stand, and market-side entry.
  • Gate 2: Hospital/clinic side and nearby residential blocks.
  • Gate 3: Bus stop, feeder services, and office corridor approach.
  • Gate 4: Mall/commercial lane, pickup-drop zone, and parking side access.
Commuter Essentials

Parking & Accessibility

BMRCL parking availability near Electronic City is generally subject to local operations and peak-hour demand.

  • Parking: BMRCL managed/authorized parking is commonly available near this station.
  • Approximate Capacity: Around 20-40 cars and 80-150 two-wheelers, varying by site layout.
  • Divyangjan Access: Lifts, escalators, tactile paths, and step-free entry are provided at or near concourse/platform levels.

Amenities at Electronic City may vary by gate and concourse side; common facilities include:

  • Public Utilities: Public restrooms, customer help desk, and security assistance point.
